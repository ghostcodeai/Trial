Because Silence Is Not An Option

Sawti is an independent journalism platform dedicated to amplifying voices across borders, languages, and communities — in Arabic, English, and French.

Our Mission

Sawti — meaning "my voice" in Arabic — was founded on one conviction: silence is not an option when injustice, change, and human stories are at stake.

We exist to give voice to the voiceless, report without fear or favour, and connect Arab diaspora communities with journalism that matters. Operating from Paris and covering stories across the Arab world, Sawti brings together a multilingual team of journalists, editors, and analysts who believe quality journalism is a public good — not a luxury.
